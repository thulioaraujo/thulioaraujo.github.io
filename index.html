I am a PhD student in Machine Learning Group at Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto since Jan 2014. My advisor is Prof. Raquel Urtasun. Prior to that, I spent one semester at Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago. I received my master degree in pattern recognition and bachelor degree in computational science, both from Northwestern Polytechnical University (2006-2013). During 2011-2013, I worked at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University as a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Lei Zhang. I worked at National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, Chinese Academy of Sciences as a research intern in Prof. Stan Li's group from 2009-2010. I was a research intern with the i3D group Microsoft Research working on the Holoportation project in the summer of 2015. My general research interest lies in computer vision and machine learning.
